FAQ's

  1. How do I use Product Genie?
    • Type in the name of the product you would like to find prices and reviews for with the search box in the top right of the home page.

  2. How does Product Genie work?
    • Upon searching for a product, Genie will find 10 different results so that you can find the best prices for you with written reviews for the product you search for.

  3. Where does Genies' information come from?
    • Product Genie uses 3 API's(Aliseeks products, Aliseeks reviews, and Contextual Web) to search from a large variety of other sites to find different vendors and reviews.

  4. Who should use this?
    • Anyone that wants to search for a specific product and get a wide variety of different results and reviews.
