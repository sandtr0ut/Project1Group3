FAQ's

  1. How do I use Product Genie?
    • Type in the name of the product you would like to find prices and reviews for with the search box in the top right of the home page.

  2. How does Product Genie work?
    • Upon searching for a product, Genie will find 20 different results from different sources so that you can find the best prices for you with written and video reviews for the product you search for. You can also use the price filter dropdown to narrow down your search based on how much is in your budget.

  3. Where does Genies' information come from?
    • Product Genie uses 3 API's(Aliseeks, Google, Youtube) to search from a large variety of other sites to find different vendors and reviews.

  4. Who should use this?
    • Anyone that is looking for a specific product and is still trying to figure out which vendor to purchase it from or that is looking to search within a certain price range.
